About Zhihao Xia

Zhihao Xia is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Fudan University. This page summarizes Zhihao Xia's education, academic profile, honors, service, skills, and contact information.

Profile

Academic Profile

Zhihao Xia is a Ph.D. student at the Institute of AI and Robotics, Academy for Engineering and Technology, Fudan University, advised by Prof. Dan Zhang. Zhihao Xia's work studies the design, synthesis, and performance analysis of generalized parallel mechanisms and robotic systems.

Zhihao Xia's research interests include generalized parallel mechanisms, generative design for robotic mechanisms, AI agents, large language models, embodied intelligence, and vision-language-action systems for intelligent manipulation.
