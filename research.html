Research

Zhihao Xia's research connects mechanism synthesis, robotic morphology, and embodied intelligence. The projects below summarize representative directions from generalized parallel mechanisms to humanoid robotic systems.

Humanoid Robotics

Guanghua Humanoid Robot Development

Role: founding team member and technical lead Dec. 2023 -- Dec. 2024

Participated in the development of Guanghua-0 and Guanghua-1, Fudan University's general-purpose humanoid robot prototypes. The work involved robotic mechanism integration, system development, and coordination between embodied-intelligence methods and physical robot design.

Generative Design

Learning-Assisted Synthesis of Generalized Parallel Mechanisms

Role: Ph.D. student researcher Sep. 2023 -- Present

Studies how mechanism synthesis can be represented as a trainable design task. This direction explores neural-network approaches, design-space representation, and computational evaluation methods for generalized parallel mechanisms.

Selected Publications

  • Zhihao Xia, Xinhua Lao, Chunxu Tian, and Dan Zhang. Mapping mechanical synthesis to a trainable task. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2026.
  • Zhihao Xia, Yongheng Xing, Chunxu Tian, and Dan Zhang. Advances in generalized parallel mechanisms. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2025.
  • Zhihao Xia, Chunxu Tian, Yongheng Xing, Jie Gu, Luquan Li, and Dan Zhang. Decoupled generalized parallel mechanisms based on motion transmission. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2025.

Mechanism Design

Humanoid-Leg-Inspired Generalized Parallel Manipulator

Role: graduate researcher 2019 -- 2022

Designed and analyzed a 6-DOF generalized parallel manipulator inspired by humanoid leg structures. The project connected topological synthesis, kinematic analysis, and humanoid robotic structure design.

Selected Output

  • Zhihao Xia, Dan Zhang, Yanyu Chen, Chunxu Tian, Jian Liu, and Chenwei Wu. A novel 6 DOFs generalized parallel manipulator design and analysis based on humanoid leg. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2022.
  • First-inventor Chinese invention patent application on a partially coupled six-DOF generalized parallel robotic leg mechanism.

Origami & Reconfiguration

Origami-Inspired and Reconfigurable Mechanism Synthesis

Role: researcher and coauthor 2023 -- Present

Explores graph-theoretic and topology-based methods for synthesizing origami-inspired, continuous-origami, configurable, and reconfigurable mechanisms for robotic applications.

Selected Publications

  • Zhihao Xia, Chunxu Tian, Luquan Li, and Dan Zhang. Continuous origami mechanisms based on topological graph theory. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2024.
  • Zhihao Xia, Chunxu Tian, Luquan Li, and Dan Zhang. Origami-inspired mechanisms based on graph theory. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2024.
  • Chunxu Tian, Luquan Li, Zhihao Xia, and Dan Zhang. Reconfigurable generalized parallel manipulators with kinematic redundancy. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2024.

Aerial Manipulation

Generalized Parallel Mechanisms for Adaptive Landing

Role: researcher and coauthor 2020 -- Present

Investigates generalized parallel mechanisms for adaptive landing, aerial manipulation, buffer landing systems, and configurable robotic structures with improved motion and force transmission.

Selected Publications

  • Jian Liu, Dan Zhang, Chunxu Tian, Weizhan Ma, and Zhihao Xia. Virtual parallel model for the buffer landing of multi-legged landing gear. Mechanism and Machine Theory, 2024.
  • Chunxu Tian, Luquan Li, Zhihao Xia, and Dan Zhang. Graph synthesis of generalized parallel mechanisms with coupling sub-chains. ICIRA, 2023.
