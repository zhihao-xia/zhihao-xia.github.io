Welcome to Zhihao Xia's academic website. Zhihao Xia works on generalized parallel mechanisms, learning-assisted mechanism synthesis, AI agents, large language models, generative robotic morphology, and embodied robotic systems.

Zhihao Xia's research aims to connect classical mechanism design with trainable computational methods, enabling robotic structures that are easier to synthesize, analyze, and deploy in intelligent physical systems.
