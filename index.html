Welcome to Zhihao Xia's Academic Website!

Zhihao Xia is a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Dan Zhang. Zhihao Xia's research interests include generalized parallel mechanisms, generative design for robotic mechanisms, embodied intelligence, and vision-language-action systems for intelligent manipulation.

Zhihao Xia's research aims to connect mechanism design with intelligent robotic decision-making. Zhihao Xia's recent work studies the synthesis, analysis, and learning-assisted design of generalized parallel mechanisms, with applications in humanoid robots, adaptive robotic systems, and next-generation embodied agents.
